Filet Mignon with Rich Balsamic Glaze

Today, we'll be making a widely popular dish all across the world which is steak. But to be more specific, this steak originated from the French with its popular name the "Filet Mignon"!

This recipe is intended to create a serving size of 2 and will roughly take 15 minutes, where 5 of those minutes will be in preparing the dish, and another 10 will be in cooking it.
