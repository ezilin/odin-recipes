Summer Berry Parfait with Yogurt and Granola

Today, I'll be showing you how to make a healthy yet delicious parfait with the main stars yogurt and gronola bars! Impress your friends with this cold, delighted treat in this hot summer!

This recipe will take roughly 10 minutes to make! No cooking or anything that complicated needed!

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup sliced strawberries
  • 3/4 cup blueberries
  • 1 (6 ounce) container vanilla yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon wheat gram
  • 1/2 banana, sliced
  • 1/3 cup granola

Steps

  1. Layer 1/4 strawberries
  2. Layer 1/4 cup blueberries
  3. Layer 1/3 container yogurt
  4. Layer 1/3 tablespoon wheat germ
  5. Place 1/3 of the sliced banana
  6. Layer about 2 tablespoons of granola in a large bowl
  7. Repeat this process until all ingredents are used
